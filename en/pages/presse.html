Pratique des Arts

In 2019, Michel Phelippeau appeared on the cover of Pratique des Arts (issue no. 149), alongside a 6-page feature on his work.

In this interview, Michel Phelippeau looks back on his journey: his training as a chemist-perfumer (a "nose"), his move to Grasse in 1990, and the connection he draws between composing a scent and composing a painting. He details his working method — his "angle method," his handling of warm and cool colours, his palette technique — as well as the workshops he runs each summer in Provence and, more recently, in Myanmar. The article also illustrates, in seven steps, the complete creation of a painting depicting the town of Grasse.
